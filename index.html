NCT 127

NCT 127 is the first fixed and second overall sub-unit of the South Korean idol group NCT, formed and managed by SM Entertainment. They debuted on July 7, 2016 with the single "Firetruck" and an original lineup of seven members: Taeil, Taeyong, Yuta, Jaehyun, Winwin, Mark, and Haechan. Since then, the lineup has changed to include three more members: Johnny, Doyoung, and Jungwoo. Winwin joined WayV, the China-based sub-unit of NCT, and has not particpated in recent comebacks.
